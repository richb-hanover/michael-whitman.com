Lyme, New Hampshire

March 2024: I have retired from making caskets and urns after more than 40 years. I have tried to find a successor, but so far no luck. There seem to be quite a few internet hits for “pine caskets,” but a lot of them are aren’t very attractive, and you should also look carefully at how well made they are, as many are mass-produced.

Some funeral homes may be able to suggest a local craftsman who does good work.

Thank you,

Michael Whitman
