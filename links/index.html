Links of interest to funeral consumers




Funeral Consumers Alliance: www.funerals.org*
The best website for straightforward information, without promoting funeral-industry interests.
  
Federal Trade Commission's excellent guide for consumers: www.ftc.gov/funerals
  
Consumer watchdog: www.FuneralEthics.org
  
Inspiringly beautiful headstones: www.MemorialsByArtists.co.uk
  
Home funerals planning: www.crossings.net and finalpassages.org
  
For Jewish traditions: www.jewish-funerals.org
  
Green burials -- simpler, less toxic, less expensive: info@greenburialcouncil.org
  
A collection of memorial songs: www.beforetheirtime.org
 
*Notes:  URLs work fine with all-lowercase letters; UpperCase as used above is for clarity.
               An ending of "dot.org" in a URL (website address) indicates a non-profit organization.
               A "dot.com" or "dot.biz" URL indicates a company in business to make a profit.
